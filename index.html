Technological Innovation and Societal Attitude during the Gilded Age and Modern Time

About

Technological trends of a given period (in this case the Gilded Age and Modern Time) cannot be viewed in isolation, but must be understeood in connection with the trends that preceded it. It must also be understood in relation with societal attitudes of the time.

This website displays a brief overview of the events and technologies that led to the periods to be studied. More information can be found in this paper (click here).
